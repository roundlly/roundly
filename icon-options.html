Pick your chair

All side-view (most recognizable shape for a chair at icon size). Each one shown big (120px) and in real card context (72px).
Side view chosen because at 40px a front-view chair flattens into a frame/box shape (which is what made the first attempt read as a bench). Side view gives the chair an instantly readable silhouette — same reason 🪑 emoji, Phosphor "Chair" icon, and IKEA's app all draw chairs sideways.
