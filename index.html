Roundlly
Made for friends at the table. Roundlly is a party game for groups in the same room — cafe, kitchen, road trip. Each player on their own phone. One person's in the hot seat, everyone else gives clues. Fastest guess wins. No online strangers, no random matchmaking. Just the people sitting next to you.
